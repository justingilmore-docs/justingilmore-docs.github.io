# Professional Standards and Methodology

### Technical Accuracy
Technical accuracy is my highest priority. I ensure all content reflects the reality of the system and is objectively verified.

### Scalable Architecture
I treat documentation as a technical product, using modular structures to ensure long-term sustainability. This approach prevents information decay and removes the risk of outdated content becoming a liability as the system evolves.

### Ownership
I take full accountability for the documentation process, identifying gaps and solving problems independently to ensure the final product meets my standards.

### Partnership
I partner with technical experts to bridge the gap between complex systems and user outcomes. I practice self-sourcing, conducting independent technical discovery, to minimize impact on expert schedules and maximize the efficiency of exchange.

### Direct Communication
I value direct communication, precise feedback, and efficient information exchange.

### Deep Work
I structure my schedule to accommodate the high cognitive demand of complex systems and optimize my workflow for maximum technical output.
